SUMMARY
2
This bill establishes municipal cost components for state and county services provided 
3
to the unorganized territory that would normally be paid for by a municipality.  The 
4
municipal cost components constitute the property tax for the unorganized territory.  The 
5
bill also provides for a one-time prepayment in fiscal year 2025-26 to the unorganized 
6
territories of Washington County and requires recovery of the prepayment through the 
7
municipal cost component on or before June 30, 2027.
