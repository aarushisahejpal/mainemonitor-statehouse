SUMMARY
14
This bill requires the Department of Environmental Protection, in consultation with the 
15
Department of Public Safety, Office of the State Fire Marshal, to design and, by July 1, 
16
2027, implement a take-back and disposal program for firefighting and fire-suppressing 
17
foam to which perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances have been intentionally added 
18
that is located in the State and that is in the possession of a person located in the State.  The 
19
program must provide for the collection of such firefighting or fire-suppressing foam from 
20
a person that voluntarily requests collection and for the safe and contained disposal of the 
21
collected foam.  The department may contract with a 3rd-party entity to implement and 
22
administer the program and may adopt rules as necessary for the implementation and 
23
administration of the program.
