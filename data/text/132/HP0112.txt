SUMMARY
16
This bill repeals the provision of law that makes it a Class E crime for a criminal 
17
defendant who has been granted preconviction or post-conviction bail to violate any 
18
condition of release. It also repeals a cross-reference to this provision. 
19
The bill does not eliminate the Class C crime for violating conditions of release.
