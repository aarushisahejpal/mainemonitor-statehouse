SUMMARY
16
This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to issue a report to 
17
the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services on an evaluation of the 
18
feasibility of upgrading the database for the municipal general assistance program to allow 
19
the determination of whether a disqualified applicant is seeking assistance in another 
20
municipality. If the department determines that the current database cannot be upgraded, 
21
the department is to research a suitable replacement database that fulfills the criterion. The 
22
department is directed to provide the committee a timeline for implementation of the 
23
upgrade or new database.
