§1730-B.  Health Information Technology Fund
4
The Health Information Technology Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund," is 
5
established within the department. 
6
1.  Use of fund.  The fund must be used to support the operation and sustainability of 
7
a state-designated statewide health information exchange operated in accordance with 
8
section 1711-C, subsection 18 and to meet the State's matching fund requirements to obtain 
9
federal funding to support the operation and sustainability of such an exchange.  Funds 
10
received pursuant to subsection 2 may be used only for purposes directly related to the 
11
operation and sustainability of a state-designated statewide health information exchange.  
12
The department shall administer the fund in a manner that maximizes eligibility for 
13
available federal matching funds.
14
2.  Source of funds; separate account; interest; nonlapsing fund.  The fund is 
15
administered by the department.  The fund consists of money appropriated or allocated by 
16
the Legislature, federal funds, grants, gifts and other funds from public or private sources.  
17
The fund must be held separate and apart from all other money, funds and accounts.  
18
Interest earned by the fund must be credited to the fund.  Any unexpended balances 
19
remaining in the fund at the end of the fiscal year do not lapse and must be carried forward 
20
to the next fiscal year. 
21

SUMMARY
29
This bill establishes the Health Information Technology Fund within the Department 
30
of Health and Human Services to support the operation and sustainability of a state-
31
designated statewide health information exchange and to meet the State's matching fund 
32
requirements to obtain available federal funding for that purpose.  The fund is nonlapsing 
33
and consists of money appropriated or allocated by the Legislature, federal funds, grants, 
34
gifts and other funds from public or private sources. 
39
The bill includes an ongoing appropriation of $1,800,000 annually in support of the 
40
fund beginning in fiscal year 2026-27.
