§14.  Community Partnerships with Public School Systems Grant Program
4

5

SUMMARY
19
This bill establishes the Community Partnerships with Public School Systems Grant 
20
Program in the Department of Education to encourage the creation of partnerships between 
21
public and private colleges and universities, community action agencies or equivalent 
22
agencies and public school systems.  It requires the program to provide grants for the 
23
purpose of offering services and addressing issues related to mental health, public health, 
24
staff development or other issues identified by the community or schools.  It also makes a 
25
one-time appropriation of $500,000 in fiscal year 2025-26 to support the program.
