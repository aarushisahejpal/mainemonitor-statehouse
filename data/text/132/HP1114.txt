SUMMARY
31
This bill directs the Commissioner of Marine Resources to allocate one scallop 
32
dragging license each for certain island communities to be issued to an applicant who is a 
33
resident of each community. The bill prohibits a license issued to a resident of an island 
34
community from being transferred to a resident of a community other than the community 
35
to which the commissioner allocated the license.  The bill also allows the commissioner to 
36
reallocate a license that goes unissued for 3 consecutive years to another island community 
37
and directs the commissioner to work with the municipal officers of the island communities 
38
and relevant organizations within the communities to implement and administer the 
39
provisions of the bill.
