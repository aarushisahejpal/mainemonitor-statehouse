SUMMARY
18
This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Marine Resources to adopt emergency rules 
19
under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 8054 to limit the taking of marine 
20
organisms by method or location to protect certain marine mammals when the 
21
commissioner determines that immediate action is necessary to mitigate circumstances 
22
posing an exceptionally high level of risk to those mammals. The bill also requires the 
23
Department of Marine Resources to submit a report to the joint standing committee of the 
24
Legislature having jurisdiction over marine resources matters no later than December 1, 
25
2027 that includes information and findings and recommendations related to any uses of 
26
the emergency rule-making authority authorized by this legislation.
