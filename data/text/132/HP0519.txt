5
§7301.  Recognition of Kineo St. John Tribe
6
1.  Recognition.  The Kineo St. John Tribe is recognized by the State as a Native 
7
American tribe.
8
2.  Effect of recognition.  Recognition of the Kineo St. John Tribe may not be 
9
construed to create, extend or form the basis of any right or claim to land or real estate in 
10
the State or any right to conduct gambling activities prohibited by law.  The Kineo St. John 
11
Tribe and the individual members of the tribe remain subject to all the laws of the State.
12
SUMMARY
13
This bill provides for state recognition of the Kineo St. John Tribe as a Native 
14
American tribe.  Recognition does not create, extend or form the basis of any right or claim 
15
to land or real estate in the State or any right to conduct gambling activities prohibited by 
16
law.  The Kineo St. John Tribe and the individual members of the tribe remain subject to 
17
all the laws of the State.
