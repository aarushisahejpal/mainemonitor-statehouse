SUMMARY
9
This bill requires school boards to adopt and implement a written policy establishing 
10
minimum and maximum temperature standards for school buildings. It requires the policy 
11
to be posted on the school board's school administrative unit's publicly accessible website. 
12
It also requires the school board to seek guidance from the Department of Education prior 
13
to adopting and implementing the written policy.
