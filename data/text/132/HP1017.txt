§2423-G.  Medical cannabis farmers' markets
4
Notwithstanding any provision of this chapter to the contrary and in accordance with 
5
the requirements of this section, the office may issue a license authorizing a person to hold 
6

7
1.  Definition.  As used in this section, "medical cannabis farmers' market" or "farmers' 
8
market" means a building, structure or place, used by one or more registered caregivers or 
9
registered dispensaries and licensed by the office in accordance with this section, for the 
10

11
consumption of harvested cannabis and cannabis products purchased by qualifying patients 
12

13

14

15

17

18

A.  Provisions regarding the application process and application requirements for 
20
issuance of a medical cannabis farmers' market license, including, but not limited to:
21
(1)  Information regarding the proposed date or dates, times and location of the 
22
farmers' market; and
23
(2)  Demonstration by the applicant of the applicant's valid registry identification 
24

25

26

27
B.  Provisions establishing criteria for issuance of a medical cannabis farmers' market 
28

29

30

31

SUMMARY
37
This bill amends the Maine Medical Use of Cannabis Act to provide for the licensure 
38
of medical cannabis farmers' markets.  The bill defines "medical cannabis farmers' market" 
39
as a building, structure or place, used by one or more registered caregivers or registered 
40
dispensaries and licensed by the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, 
41
Office of Cannabis Policy, for the direct sale of harvested cannabis and cannabis products 
42
to qualifying patients and for the consumption of harvested cannabis and cannabis products 
43
purchased by qualifying patients from the registered caregivers or registered dispensaries.
