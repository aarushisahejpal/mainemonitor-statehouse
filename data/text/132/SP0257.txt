SUMMARY
15
This bill amends the provision of law regarding licensure as a professional engineer for 
16
a person holding an active national council record to make that provision consistent with 
17
other licensure provisions. It provides that an applicant for licensure as a professional 
18
engineer who meets the requirements of a mutual recognition agreement between this State 
19
and another state, territory or possession of the United States, the District of Columbia or 
20
any foreign country, whose licensure qualifications are, in the opinion of the State Board 
21
of Licensure for Professional Engineers and by the language of the agreement, substantially 
22
equivalent to the requirements of the laws of this State, may be licensed without further 
23
examination.
