SUMMARY
22
This bill adds marine finfish aquaculture facilities and general permit coverage for 
23
municipal separate storm sewer systems as waste discharge license groups and establishes 
24
the basis for the annual fees for those license groups.  It changes the basis for the annual 
25
fee for the general permit coverage for the marine aquaculture facilities waste discharge 
26
license group and increases the median fee for that license group.  It also clarifies that the 
27
fish-rearing facility waste discharge license group covers terrestrial fish-rearing facilities.
