§4408.  Lifetime ferry passes for retired ferry service employees
4
A state employee as defined in Title 5, section 17001, subsection 40 who is retired from 
5
employment with the Maine State Ferry Service is eligible to receive a lifetime ferry pass 
6
and a waiver from any reservation fees. For the purposes of this section, "lifetime ferry 
7
pass" means a pass that provides, on a ferry operated by the Maine State Ferry Service, 2 
8
round-trip passages per month for a passenger and a vehicle if the passenger gives at least 
9
24 hours' notice of a planned round-trip passage to the Maine State Ferry Service.
10
SUMMARY
11
This bill provides that a state employee who is retired from employment with the Maine 
12
State Ferry Service is eligible to receive a lifetime ferry pass and a waiver from any 
13
reservation fees. The bill defines "lifetime ferry pass" as a pass that provides 2 round-trip 
14
passages per month for a passenger and a vehicle on a ferry operated by the Maine State 
15
Ferry Service if the passenger gives at least 24 hours' notice of a planned round-trip passage 
16
to the Maine State Ferry Service.
