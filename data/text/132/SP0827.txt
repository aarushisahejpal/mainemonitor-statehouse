Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §5805, sub-§3-A is enacted to read:
3
3-A.  Maine School of Science and Mathematics.  A sending unit that does not 
4
operate or contract with a secondary school shall pay the cost of attendance, including room 
5
and board, for a student in that sending unit to attend the Maine School of Science and 
6
Mathematics.  The maximum allowable cost of attendance at the Maine School of Science 
7
and Mathematics is the actual room and board rate determined by the Board of Trustees of 
8
the Maine School of Science and Mathematics or the state average per public secondary 
9
student cost as adjusted, whichever is lower, multiplied by the local contribution percentage 
10
of the essential programs and services amounts, as determined by the most recent report of 
11
the Department of Education, for that sending unit.  The school board of the sending unit 
12
may vote to pay a higher cost of attendance rate.

SUMMARY
2
This bill requires a sending school administrative unit that does not operate or contract 
3
with a secondary school to pay the cost of attending the Maine School of Science and 
4
Mathematics for students from the unit and specifies that the cost of attending the Maine 
5
School of Science and Mathematics includes room and board. The bill also provides 
6
funding to the Maine School of Science and Mathematics to support administrative 
7
operations, to support in-state students' room and board expenses and for infrastructure 
8
upgrades to the school's dormitory.
