SUMMARY
14
This bill directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
15
General Services to ensure that each state building is equipped with at least one automated 
16
external defibrillator.  It also directs each municipality to ensure that each municipal 
17
building is equipped with at least one automated external defibrillator.
