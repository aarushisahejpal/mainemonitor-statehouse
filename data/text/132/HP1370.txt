SUMMARY
10
This bill directs the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, in 
11
partnership with outside agencies or other state agencies as necessary, to institute, with 
12
input of the commissioner's advisory committee to guide the implementation of the State's 
13
strategic plan to end hunger in the State by 2030, an annual survey and regularly publish a 
14
report regarding food insecurity in the State.
