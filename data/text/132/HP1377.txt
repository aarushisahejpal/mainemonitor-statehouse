Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §2054, sub-§12 is enacted to read:
9
12.  Variable-message signs.  The operator of an authorized public service vehicle 
10

11

12
recovery or general traffic control. The Commissioner of Public Safety may adopt rules as 
13
necessary for the purpose of implementing this subsection. Rules adopted pursuant to this 
14
subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
