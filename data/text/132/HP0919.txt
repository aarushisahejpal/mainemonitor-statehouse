SUMMARY
14
This bill amends the law governing the authority of the Department of Health and 
15
Human Services under the Child and Family Services and Child Protection Act. The bill 
16
requires that the department provide funding for community-based organizations to 
17
administer certain family-centered programs that support positive childhood experiences 
18
and lead to increased social and economic mobility for families.
