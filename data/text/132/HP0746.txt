SUMMARY
8
This bill specifies that a receiver appointed by the Public Utilities Commission to 
9
oversee the operations of a consumer-owned water utility must be treated in the same 
10
manner as an employee acting on behalf of a governmental entity under the Maine Tort 
11
Claims Act. This change provides a receiver, who acts in place of trustees, with the same 
12
immunity accorded to trustees.
