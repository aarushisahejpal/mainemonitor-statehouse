SUMMARY
23
This bill, which is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 
24
Natural Resources pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 2124-A, 
25
subsection 2, implements recommendations of the Department of Environmental 
26
Protection included in the department's annual report regarding solid waste generation and 
27
disposal capacity.  The bill amends financial assurance requirements for certain solid waste 
28
processing facilities and solid waste disposal facilities to align those requirements with 
29
federal regulations governing financial assurance criteria for solid waste facilities adopted 
30
by the United States Environmental Protection Agency.
31
The committee has not taken a position on the substance of the bill and by reporting 
32
this bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees 
33
or disagrees with any aspect of this bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole 
34
purpose of obtaining a printed bill that can be referred to the committee for a public hearing 
35
and subsequent committee action in the normal course.
