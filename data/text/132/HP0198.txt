SUMMARY
2
This bill creates 3 Behavioral Health Coordinator positions assigned to the state police 
3
Southern Field Troop, Central Field Troop and Troop F responsible for making decisions 
4
about the health, safety and welfare of persons in the community who have interacted with 
5
law enforcement professionals and are in need of services. The bill also creates one 
6
Behavioral Health Coordinator Supervisor position to oversee the activities of the 
7
Behavioral Health Coordinator positions and coordinate with the state police field troops.
