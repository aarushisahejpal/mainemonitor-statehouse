Sec. 1.  35-A MRSA §10314 is enacted to read:
3
§10314.  Maine Home Energy Navigator and Coaching Resource Hub
4
The Maine Home Energy Navigator and Coaching Resource Hub, referred to in this 
5
section as "the resource hub," is established within the department to promote community-
6
based energy coaching services to residential consumers that provide guidance, particularly 
7
to those in low-income, rural and underserved communities, to understand and navigate 
8
energy efficiency and clean energy investment options to affordably meet their home 
9
energy needs. Through the resource hub, the department shall make available standardized 
10
training curricula and program materials that may be used by communities to implement 
11
their own energy coaching programs. The department, as funding allows, shall administer 
12
the resource hub.

SUMMARY
21
This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 
22
Technology pursuant to Resolve 2025, chapter 86, section 2. The bill establishes the Maine 
23
Home Energy Navigator and Coaching Resource Hub within the Department of Energy 
24
Resources. Through the resource hub, the department must make available standardized 
25
training curricula and program materials to support communities in implementing their own 
26
energy coaching programs. The bill makes a one-time appropriation of $100,000 in fiscal 
27
year 2026-27 to provide funding for the resource hub.
32
The committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill. By reporting this 
33
bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or 
34

35

36
public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course. The committee is taking 
37
this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the proposals 
38
