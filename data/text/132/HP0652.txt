SUMMARY
13
This bill allows a municipal fire department to conduct a sprinkler plan review if the 
14
municipal employee conducting the review is certified as a plan reviewer by the National 
15
Fire Protection Association and the municipal fire department establishes a review process 
16
that includes a thorough evaluation of a plan to ensure compliance with all applicable codes 
17
and standards.
