program.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall contract 
3
with an independent service provider with expertise in foster care and extended care and 
4

5

6
section 4037-A. The contracted independent service provider shall research mentoring 
7

8

9

10

11
independent service provider shall consult with youth who currently have extended care 
12

13

14

15

16
human services matters, no later than January 2, 2027, with recommendations for a 
17
mentoring program.  The report must include the recommendations from the contracted 
18
independent service provider, descriptions of youth viewpoints and the department's 
19
response to the recommendations.  The joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
20
jurisdiction over health and human services matters is authorized to report out legislation 
21
related to the report to the 133rd Legislature in 2027.

SUMMARY
23
This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to contract with 
24
an independent service provider to design a mentoring program for youth who have 
25
extended care and support agreements with the department.  The contract includes 
26
researching mentoring programs that are evidence-based, training requirements for 
27
participants and data collection and analysis.  The input of youth who have or have had 
28
extended care and support agreements must be specifically included.  The department must 
29
submit a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
30
health and human services matters, no later than January 2, 2027, with recommendations 
31
for a mentoring program.  The committee is authorized to report out legislation to the 133rd 
32
Legislature in 2027.
