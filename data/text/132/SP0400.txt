SUMMARY
19
This bill requires a health insurance carrier, beginning in 2026, to provide a quarterly 
20
report to the Superintendent of Insurance that identifies the number of claims for that 
21
quarter that were denied, the number of claims for that quarter for which prior authorization 
22
was denied, the 5 most common reasons for a claim denial and the 5 most common reasons 
23
for a prior authorization denial.  The superintendent is required to submit an annual report 
24
on that information as well as information provided to the superintendent by the United 
25
States Department of Health and Human Services regarding claim and prior authorization 
26
denials under the federal Affordable Care Act to the joint standing committee of the 
27
Legislature having jurisdiction over health coverage, insurance and financial services 
28
matters.  The committee is authorized to submit legislation related to the annual report to 
29
the session of the Legislature in which the annual report is received.
