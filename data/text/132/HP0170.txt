§458.  Appearance of counsel by remote methods
4
Unless justice requires otherwise, the court shall schedule pretrial nontestimonial 
5
criminal matters in a manner that permits the appearance of counsel by remote methods to 
6
the greatest extent possible. As used in this section, "remote methods" has the same 
7
meaning as in Title 1, section 403-B, subsection 1.
8
SUMMARY
9
This bill provides that, unless justice requires otherwise, the court must schedule 
10
pretrial nontestimonial criminal matters in a manner that permits the appearance of counsel 
11
by remote methods to the greatest extent possible.
