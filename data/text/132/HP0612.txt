Sec. 1.  23 MRSA §4401, sub-§1 is enacted to read:
3
1.  Overnight docking on islands of ferry vessels.  The Department of Transportation

5

SUMMARY
16
This bill requires the Department of Transportation to allow a ferry operated by the 
17
Maine State Ferry Service to dock overnight at its island port or to dock at a mainland port 
18
when that docking is common practice, with the exception that the department may require 
19
a ferry vessel to dock at a mainland port or at an alternative island port for an emergency 
20
situation, including, but not limited to, an extreme weather event.
