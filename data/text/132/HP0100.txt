SUMMARY
19
This bill requires the MaineCare program to provide reimbursement for a hearing aid 
20
for each hearing-impaired ear of an individual enrolled in the MaineCare program. Under 
21
current law, the MaineCare program provides reimbursement for a hearing aid for one 
22
hearing-impaired ear of an individual enrolled in the MaineCare program, with additional 
23
requirements as established by rule for coverage of a hearing aid for the 2nd hearing-
24
impaired ear.
