SUMMARY
8
This bill amends the law governing protection from abuse orders to give the court, 
9
beginning January 1, 2026, the authority to issue a final protection order directing the 
10
defendant to refrain from knowingly being within a specified distance of the plaintiff or 
11
prohibiting the defendant from being present at a specific location.
