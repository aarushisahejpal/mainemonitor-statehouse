Sec. 1. Surplus property identified for redevelopment.  Resolved:  That, by 
2
October 1, 2025, each state agency and semiautonomous state agency shall submit to the 
3
Maine Redevelopment Land Bank Authority, established in the Maine Revised Statutes, 
4
Title 30-A, section 5154 and referred to in this section as "the authority," a list of properties 
5
owned by the agency that the agency has determined to be surplus and potentially eligible 
6
for redevelopment under Title 30-A, section 5157.  The authority shall provide to each 
7
agency the information that must be included for each property identified on the agency's 
8
list.  By December 3, 2025, the authority shall submit a report to the Joint Standing 
9
Committee on Housing and Economic Development.  The report must include the 
10
authority's findings regarding the listed properties and the potential for redevelopment of 
11
those properties, including redevelopment for housing and economic development 
12
purposes.  For the purposes of this section, "semiautonomous state agency" has the same 
13
meaning as in Title 5, section 1972, subsection 9.
14
SUMMARY
15
This resolve requires each state agency and each agency created by an act of the 
16
Legislature that is not a part of the Executive Department to submit to the Maine 
17
Redevelopment Land Bank Authority a list of properties owned by the agency that the 
18
agency has determined to be surplus and potentially eligible for redevelopment under the 
19
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 5157.  It also requires the Maine 
20
Redevelopment Land Bank Authority to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee 
21
on Housing and Economic Development regarding the properties and their potential for 
22
redevelopment.
