SUMMARY
20
This bill provides that a county or municipal law enforcement officer or a municipal 
21
firefighter who is employed in the position of a county or municipal law enforcement 
22
officer or a municipal firefighter and who previously elected not to enroll in the Retired 
23
County and Municipal Law Enforcement Officers and Municipal Firefighters Health 
24
Insurance Program may enroll from October 1, 2025 to November 30, 2025. The bill also 
25
requires the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Human 
26
Resources, Office of Employee Health and Wellness to provide electronic briefings 
27
regarding the program every 2 months to newly hired county or municipal law enforcement 
28
officers or municipal firefighters who are eligible to enroll in the program.
