SUMMARY
15
This bill establishes an exception to the standard eligibility criteria of the Department 
16
of Corrections’ supervised community confinement program by allowing a prisoner to be 
17
eligible if the prisoner is serving a term of imprisonment of at least 15 years, the prisoner 
18
has served at least 15 years of the term of imprisonment, the prisoner has continuously 
19
maintained a custody classification of medium, medium trustee status or minimum for the 
20
5 years preceding the prisoner's consideration for the supervised community confinement 
21
program and the crime or crimes for which the prisoner is serving the term of imprisonment 
22
were committed before the prisoner attained 26 years of age.
