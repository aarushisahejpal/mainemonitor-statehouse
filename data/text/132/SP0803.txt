SUMMARY
12
This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Marine Resources to require an educational 
13
course for the issuance or renewal of a seaweed permit.  If the educational course 
14
requirement applies only to certain species of seaweed, the commissioner may require an 
15
endorsement on the seaweed permit for those species, which must be made available at no 
16
